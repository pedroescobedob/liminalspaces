1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to site owners.

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

About the Game

Liminal Spaces is a first-person exploration horror game that drops you into the uncanny spaces between reality. Navigate through backrooms, abandoned metro stations, sci-fi corridors, and medical wards.

Each map is a labyrinth. You spawn in a bed cell and must explore to find the hidden escape point. But you're not alone - alien creatures, zombie nurses, and other entities patrol these forgotten places.

Built with Unreal Engine 5, featuring Nanite, Lumen global illumination, and atmospheric post-processing that makes every flickering hallway feel alive.

Features

☉

Liminal Environments

Backrooms, abandoned metros, sci-fi hallways, medical wards, and underground facilities. Each map is a unique liminal experience.

⚔

Combat System

Equip weapons to defend yourself. Hitscan and projectile weapons with ammo management. Defeat enemies to survive.

☠

Hostile Entities

Zombie nurses patrol medical wards. Alien creatures stalk sci-fi corridors. Each enemy has unique AI behavior and attack patterns.

⛟

Atmospheric Horror

Flickering fluorescent lights, chromatic aberration, film grain, and ambient soundscapes create an unsettling atmosphere.

⚡

Escape Objective

Spawn in a bed cell. Explore the map. Find the hidden escape point. Race against time and compete on the global leaderboard.

☁

Cross-Platform

Play on PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, iOS, and Android. Your progress syncs across devices.
